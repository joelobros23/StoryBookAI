Privacy Policy for Storybook AI

Last Updated: July 22, 2024

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Storybook AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password.
  • Facebook Login: If you choose to log in with Facebook, we receive your public profile information, which includes your name and email address, as permitted by you through the Facebook permissions dialog.
  • User-Generated Content: Any stories, prompts, images, and other content you create within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate you when you log in.
  • Communicate with you about your account or our services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your data is stored securely and is only used to provide the functionality of the Storybook AI application.

User Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your Storybook AI account and all related data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to laberintojoelo@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, please state your full name and confirm that you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

We will process your request and confirm the deletion of your data via email within 30 days. Please note that this action is irreversible and will result in the permanent loss of all your created stories and account information.
